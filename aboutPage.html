About Us


CobaltGaming is our ecommerce channel with the core focus of bringing the world’s leading gaming entertainment content and products to our loyal customers with an amazing experience from the moment you arrive on the website and place an order to when it arrives at your door.


The CobaltGaming team has many years of experience in gaming and filmed entertainment content, and always strives to fulfil the needs of our customers. This team have strong and valued relationships with most of our key partners and principals, most of which have spanned across two decades such as Sony Pictures Releasing International, Sony Interactive Entertainment Europe and The Walt Disney Company.


Beyond those listed above some of the brands we represent in the gaming entertainment space include Warner Bros Gaming, Bethesda, Konami, Techland, Milestone, Capcom, SEGA, and Big Ben.
